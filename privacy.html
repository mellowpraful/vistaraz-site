How we protect your privacy

Privacy isn't a feature here — it's the foundation. Judges ask, we show.
ANONYMOUS BY DESIGN

No name, email, or phone

You're identified only by an opaque anon_id. Layers 1–2 need zero personal data to work.

ZERO FACULTY / PARENT VIEW

Counselor booking is blind

College admin sees only your anonymous ID + time slot. No link to your student record, faculty, or family.

LOCAL-FIRST (DEMO)

Your data stays on device

In this demo, all check-in state lives in your browser's localStorage. Nothing leaves the device.

AUTO-PURGE

Logs expire

Production conversation logs are hashed and auto-deleted after a short TTL — we keep what helps, not what hunts.

CONSENT-GATED

Identities revealed by you

Peer/counselor identities are shown only with mutual consent. You control when to step into the light.

ENCRYPTED

Transit + rest

TLS in transit, column-level encryption at rest for any linkage data in the production build.

The trust promise

We designed MANNMITRA so the safest thing to do is also the easiest: open the app, check in, get routed — all without ever "outing" yourself to an institution. Help scales only as far as trust allows, so we put trust first.
