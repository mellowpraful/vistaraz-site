Why this matters

Students do not need more awareness. They need a safer path to action.

The barrier is not only supply. It is cost, shame, confusion, and the friction of figuring out who to trust. This platform removes that friction with one consistent front door.

Design principle

Start with empathy, then route with precision.

Peer support absorbs hesitation. Triage narrows urgency. Counselors handle clinical care. Campuses handle policy and continuity. The platform orchestrates all four.
